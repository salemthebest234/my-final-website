SIMPLE
OMLETTE
RECIPIE

An easy quick dish,perfect for
any meal. This classic omelette
combines beaten eggs cooked to
perfectiopn,optionally filled with your
choice of cheese,vegetable,or
meats.

preparation-time

. Total: Approximately 10
minutes

. Preparation:5minutes

. coking:5minutes

Ingredients

. 2-3Large eggs

. salt, to taste

pepper, to taste

. 1 tablespoon of butter or oil.

. Optionally fillings:cheese, diced
vegetables,cooked meats,
herbs

Instruction

1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat
the eggs with a pinch of salt and
pepper until they are well
mixed.You can add a
tablespoon of water or milk for
a fluffeier textture.

2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick
frying pan over medium heat
and add butteraor oil

3. Cook the omelette:Once the
butter is melted bubling,
pour in the eggs.Tilit the pan to
ensure the eggs evenly coat the
surface

4. Add fillings(optionally):When
the eggs begin to set at the
edges but are still slightly runny
in the middle,sprinkle your
chosen filling over one half of
the omlelette.

5. Fold and serve:As the omelette
cointinues to cook, carefully lift
one edges and fold it over half of
the omelette.

6. Enjoy:Serve hot,with additional
salt and pepper if needed.
